Your Privacy Matters

UniCast is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the essential data needed to deliver timely and relevant emergency alerts. Your location data, if enabled, is used solely for geotargeting alerts and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. All personal information is handled with the utmost care and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

How Your Data is Used

The data collected by UniCast helps us ensure that emergency messages reach you efficiently and in your preferred language. This includes anonymized usage statistics to improve app performance and reliability. We do not sell your personal data. Our commitment is to provide a public service without compromising your personal information.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We believe in clear and open communication. This "Know Your Rights" page is part of our commitment to transparency. Should you have any questions about our data practices or how UniCast operates, please refer to our full Privacy Policy (link to be added) or contact our support team. Your trust is paramount to us.
